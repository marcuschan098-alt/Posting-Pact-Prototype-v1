Section A · Career Twin
What should the Career Twin include?
Career Twin is the pre-conversation tool — members explore and war-game their own paths before the four-seat Pact. Select the components you want to include. Click to toggle.
✓
🗺️
Path Explorer
See where your choices lead
A branching map of realistic career paths from the member's current position. Pick a course, posting, or vocation and see the 3–5 year progression — built on historical career-path data.
MVP
✓
📋
Prep Brief Generator
Auto-draft for all four seats
After exploring paths, the Twin auto-drafts a short prep brief — aspirations, explored paths, top questions — sent to all four conversation seats before the Pact meeting. Replaces blank-sheet prep.
MVP
✓
📊
Peer Benchmark
How common is this path?
For each path explored, shows how many people with similar profiles took this route — and what happened to them. Grounds aspiration in real precedent, prevents both under-ambition and fantasy plans.
MVP
✅
Gap Advisor
What you need to get there
For a chosen target path, shows what qualifications, courses, or postings are required to unlock it — and which the member already has. Turns aspirations into an actionable checklist.
Optional
⚖️
What-if Simulator
Play out a specific decision
Pick a specific fork — "what if I take this posting vs. that one?" — and see both outcomes side by side: readiness implications, next likely posting, time to promotion band.
Optional
🤖
AI Path Generator
Suggest non-obvious routes
AI layer that surfaces unconventional cross-vocation paths the member wouldn't have thought to explore — cross-silo possibilities based on transferable skills. Upgrade after MVP data layer is proven.
